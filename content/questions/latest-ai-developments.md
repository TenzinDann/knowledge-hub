2024 has been an explosive year for AI development. Here's what you need to know about the latest breakthroughs and releases.

## Major Model Releases

### **GPT-4 Turbo (Q4 2023 - Still Dominant)**
- Vision capabilities (analyze images)
- 128K context window
- Improved instruction following
- Lower pricing

### **Claude 3 Opus (March 2024)**
- 200K token context (industry-leading)
- Superior code analysis
- Enhanced reasoning capabilities
- Better at following complex instructions

### **Google Gemini 1.5 (February 2024)**
- 1 million token context window
- Multimodal from the ground up
- Improved mathematical reasoning
- Integration with Google Workspace

### **Llama 3 (April 2024)**
- Meta's most capable open-source model
- 70B and 405B parameter versions
- Competitive with GPT-4 on many benchmarks
- Free for commercial use

## AI Video Generation Breakthroughs

### **OpenAI Sora (Limited Release)**
- Text-to-video generation
- Up to 60 seconds of high-quality video
- Understanding of physics and motion
- Currently in limited beta

### **Runway Gen-3 Alpha**
- Improved consistency
- Better character and scene understanding
- Available to paid users
